Growth analyst

Play a key role in growing one of London’s most exciting tech start-ups. We are looking for smart, metrics-oriented people to join our growth team.

You should have a strong interest in, and ideally experience of, A/B testing, PPC, SEO, lead nurture and other forms of digital marketing. You will join GoCardless's growth team and be responsible for building and optimsing a customer acquisition channel.

On a day-to-day basis, you will:

  • Develop and execute a strategy to hit your channel growth target
  • Track click-through rates and conversion in order to understand ROI
  • Optimise performance in order to build a scalable acquisition channel

Requirements:

  • Strong awareness of digital marketing techniques
  • Confidence with numbers & analytics
  • Experience using Google Analytics & search marketing tools
  • Great oral & written communication skills

Bonus points for:

  • Technical background including experience of basic web programming

There’s very little hierarchy, a common hunger to learn, lots of freedom and all the responsibility you want to take. There’s also a great office atmosphere, good coffee, tons of fruit and juice, Friday beers and regular dinners out.

Competitive salary ranging from £25-50k plus options dependent on experience. Roles available for recent graduates as well as people with a few years' experience of online marketing.
