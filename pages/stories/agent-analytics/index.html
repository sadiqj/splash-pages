Agent Analytics transformed its business thanks to GoCardless

“GoCardless is one of those things you set and forget – I know it’s there and I know it’s doing its job.”

Tim, founder of Agent Analytics

GoCardless helped them…

  • Grow their customer base 150%
  • Increase turnover to £350,000
  • Reduce late or overdue bills to 1%

Challenge

“We wanted a system with the ability to scale”

Agent Analytics provides business intelligence and automated marketing services to estate agents. As a startup with ambitious plans for expansion, Agent Analytics required a payment system which was able to grow with the business.

“We wanted a system with the ability to scale which was as streamlined and free of paper as possible and which could be integrated with our accounting system.”

Solution

“GoCardless offered online Direct Debit without the setup fees and manual admin”

Agent Analytics discovered GoCardless through one of our partners, Directli. “When we found out that GoCardless offered online direct debit without the set up fees and manual admin I had experienced previously and was FSA approved I was keen to sign up.”

Agent Analytics now ask all customers to pay using GoCardless and manage their Direct Debits through Directli.

Benefits

“GoCardless was one of those things that you can set and forget – I know it’s there and I know it’s doing its job”

Since joining GoCardless a year ago, Agent Analytics have grown from collecting around 20 payments a month to 300 – all without an accounts department. Their annual turnover has also grown significantly and is now £350,000.

“We are confident we can keep growing and reach 1000 customers by the end of this year with the launch of two exciting new products – and the help of GoCardless. It's one of those things that you can set and forget – I know it’s there and I know it’s doing its job. It’s an enabler and I want to keep expanding our business with it.”
