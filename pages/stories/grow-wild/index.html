Grow Wild reduced admin time by 97% by using Direct Debit with GoCardless

<%= image_tag "home/stories/grow-wild/headshot.jpg", class: 'u-border-circle'%>

“Now processing payments takes 5 minutes not 3 and a half hours.”

Lindsay Girvin, Owner, Grow Wild

GoCardless helped them…

  • Reduce admin time by 97.5%
  • Collect payments from 98% of customers using GoCardless
  • Complete payment runs in less than 5 minutes

Challenge

“We were spending at least 10 hours a week on payments”

Grow Wild is a veg box company established in 1998. Until last year they were reliant on traditional payment collection methods such as cheques, cash and cards. “This was extremely time consuming; we were spending at least 10 hours a week manually entering payments, chasing late payments and trying to update expired card details.”

Solution

“I couldn’t believe collecting payments could be so simple”

They started to think that Direct Debit might be the solution but were put off by the expense involved. “We chose GoCardless based on a recommendation from Dogmoor, another GoCardless customer. They said it’s simple and easy and brilliant so we looked into it. I couldn’t believe collecting payments could be so simple.” Now 98% of Grow Wild’s customers pay through GoCardless.

Benefits

“Processing payments takes 5 minutes rather than 3 ½ hours”

The main benefit for Lindsay has been the reduced admin time. “Before using GoCardless we were spending over ten hours a week managing the payment process. Now processing payments takes 5 minutes rather than 3 and a half hours. This frees me up to focus on diversifying and expanding the business. I can be more strategic and less operational.”

Grow Wild have found GoCardless incredibly easy to use. Lindsay explained that “it is incredibly easy for our customers to see what payments are going out. It’s also a great reference for us. If customers have queries we can go straight to GoCardless to see what’s what.”
