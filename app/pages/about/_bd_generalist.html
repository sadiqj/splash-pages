Business Development Generalist

Drive our growth and shape our future

GoCardless is poised for global success, and we need people to help us seize opportunities to achieve our full potential. In the last few months alone, we've started serving large organisations like The Guardian, TripAdvisor, and the UK Government – and we're just getting started.

We're looking for systematic problem-solvers to join our business development team and help us scale our payment system across the globe. You will plan & execute entire projects and you'll own their outcomes. You'll be responsible for developing strategy, research & analysis, building a plan and then implementing it and solving challenges until you succeed.

We work with leaders across the company on a wide variety of problems. Projects we've kicked off in 2015 so far include:

  • Working with Country Leads to develop European launch plans
  • Assessing which geographies to expand into next
  • Working with engineers to prioritise product features for multinational companies
  • Improving the onboarding process for customers

You need to be comfortable with ambiguity and a fast-changing environment, because no two weeks are the same. You'll need to be confident making recommendations, weighing opposing views and taking feedback onboard, but holding firm when the data supports your decision. You'll have the freedom & responsibility to determine your own goals and how best to achieve these.

We've built a friendly, collaborative culture, where we hire and develop ambitious people that share our desire to do work we can be proud of. We value learning and feedback, and are devoted to encouraging and supporting each other's continual professional growth. There will be significant scope for you to develop & expand your role as our team continues to grow rapidly.

You're an ideal candidate if:

  • You have strong problem-solving skills
  • You execute on the details while keeping the big picture in mind
  • You're adaptive and keen to pick up new skills
  • You're driven and self-motivated
  • You're intellectually curious & creative

Our current team come from a variety of backgrounds: mechanical engineering, economics, consulting, finance, entrepreneurship & startups. We welcome a wide diversity of candidates – if you're unsure, please apply.
