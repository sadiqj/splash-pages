Country Lead: Germany, Netherlands, Spain

Lead our international expansion.

As part of its rapid growth, GoCardless is expanding its operations internationally and we are looking for people to lead this. You will be responsible for in-market success and work directly with the founders of the company to achieve this, as well as interacting with every part of the GoCardless organisation.

Launching in a new territory requires a depth and breadth of skills akin to starting a company, and you will therefore need to be a relentless problem-solver with in-market experience who works well independently. Ideally you'll have a strong background in operating & scaling a business.

You'll be responsible for figuring out initial operational requirements pre-launch and making sure these are met. You'll design and execute a sales, marketing and partnership strategy which delivers rapid growth. As well as this, you'll be responsible for handling initial sales, support and operations — including hiring a team to support & scale in-market growth.

Strong candidates will demonstrate these qualities:

  • Self reliant: you will need to be able to drive in-country work independently and resourcefully, and grow to become the expert of your market
  • Result-oriented: starting from scratch, you will deliver on launch activities, accompany prospective customers from sales to live, and in general build momentum early on
  • Multifaceted: you will be responsible for all client-facing activities in-market (marketing, PR, sales, account management, partnerships) and be able to understand and coordinate all product, engineering and support requirements for your market success
  • Adaptable: you will need to be able to communicate complex situations in a clear and simple way to different audiences from different cultures, from the CTO of a prospective client in your local market to the VP of Marketing at GoCardless.

You should apply if you are:

  • A native speaker of the language who has lived and worked in the country
  • An independent problem-solver with a track record of getting stuff done
  • Driven, with a relentless work ethic

Bonus points for:

  • Experience hiring teams & scaling operations
  • Experience of product management
  • Experience of software sales

Industry competitive salary. You will be based in London with lots of travel to the market. This role would ideally suit someone with experience successfully operating & scaling a technology company in this market or 2-5 years' professional experience at a leading consultancy.
