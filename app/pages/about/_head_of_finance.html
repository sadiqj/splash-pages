Head of Finance

We are looking for a Head of Finance to help us build our finance capabilities as we scale our company.

We are an ambitious high-growth company that is quickly evolving. Since this time last year, our revenue has grown 450% and our team has more than doubled in size. We are working with increasingly large customers and will be expanding our operations internationally very soon.

As Head of Finance, you will report directly to our CEO and assist the management team in setting the strategic direction of the company. You will play a critical role in plotting a path for us to become the dominant recurring payments provider in Europe within the next couple of years.

You will also need to be hands on, taking charge of the day-to-day financial operations of our business. You’ll bring our accounting in-house and structure our finance function. You will be proactive, optimising our existing processes, and implementing new ones to ensure that we have full oversight of our spending and revenue and can make well-informed decisions.

Your responsibilities will include:

  • Owning the financial plan to grow our company by an order of magnitude
  • Working with the management team to identify and measure key financial metrics
  • Anticipating and addressing any financial or tax issues arising from our international expansion
  • Financial reporting for board meetings
  • Taking over responsibility for key commercial contracts

You'll also take full ownership of our accounting processes including setting   enforcing budgets across the company; preparing monthly management accounts and statutory annual accounts; optimising our treasury management.

We’re looking for someone who:

  • Is a fully qualified accountant
  • Has 3-5 years experience in a quickly evolving high-growth company
  • Has a strategic and commercial mindset
  • Communicates well
  • Is organised and attentive to detail
  • Is decisive and fact-oriented
