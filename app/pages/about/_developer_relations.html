Developer Relations

GoCardless is an engineering led company solving interesting problems across every part of the web stack. Along the way, we've learnt plenty of lessons others could benefit from.

We want to give back to the web development community, and are looking for someone to help us do that.

The role

You'll work closely with our engineering team to coordinate and organise our blogging, open source work and conference involvement. This includes but is not limited to:

  • Organising hosting and sponsoring meet-ups in our office space
  • Being aware of upcoming events and conferences that a GoCardless engineer would be suited to speaking at, and keep track of the call for proposals
  • Arranging and scheduling engineering [blog posts](https://gocardless.com/blog/tagged/engineering)

You'll also play a key part working with us to improve our community contributions and suggest new ways that GoCardless can give back and get involved with the community. You'll own and improve what we do in these areas and how we do it and get support from the rest of the company to help make it possible.

What we're looking for

  • A passion for development and some experience writing code
  • An organised individual
  • Great communication skills
  • Experience of working in an engineering focused company

In your application, please include your CV and a link to your GitHub, as well as telling us about an interesting technical project or problem that you've recently worked on.
