Developer Relations

GoCardless is an engineering led company solving interesting problems across every part of the web stack. Along the way, we've learnt plenty of lessons others could benefit from.

We want to give back to the web development community, and are looking for someone to help us do that.

The role

You'll be responsible for managing our contributions to the community, including arranging meetups and hackathons to host in our office and inviting external guests into GoCardless to do a talk.

Additionally, you'll oversee our conference efforts, tracking conferences that we can sponsor or attend, and ones we should be speaking at.

This includes being aware of upcoming conference calls for proposals, and working with engineering to submit talks that suit the conference and our skillsets.

You will also manage and own our engineering blog psts, suggest new posts to be written based off our recent work and schedule time to make sure the blog posts become a reality.

You'll play a key part working with us to improve our community contributions and suggest new ways that GoCardless can give back and get involved with the community. You'll own and improve what we do in these areas and how we do it and get support from the rest of the company to help make it possible.

What we're looking for

  • A passion for development and experience writing code
  • An organised individual
  • Great communication skills
  • Experience of working in an engineering focused company
  • Engagement with the development community - a personal blog, attending or speaking at events, or open source work

In your application, please include your CV and a link to your GitHub, as well as telling us about an interesting technical project or problem that you've recently worked on.
