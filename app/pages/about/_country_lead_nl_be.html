Country Lead: Netherlands & Belgium

The opportunity is there: now we need to move fast to become a global success. We're looking for results-oriented, adaptable problem-solvers to lead this expansion into Belgium & the Netherlands.

The role

We've laid foundations in the UK, and these will guide you as you figure out a strategy that will deliver rapid growth in the Netherlands and Belgium. Then you'll execute this strategy, driving in-country work independently and resourcefully, and swiftly becoming an expert in your market.

You will work closely with leaders across the company to understand all product, engineering and operational requirements, so you'll need to be adept at discussing complex situations with a wide variety of audiences. You'll feed your market's needs into our development roadmap to ensure we're always building the product people want.

All client-facing activities in your market will be your responsibility: planning and delivering on launch activities to build momentum early; accompanying prospective customers from sales to live. You'll handle initial marketing, PR, sales, partnerships and account management, and start to build a team to support & scale growth in your market.

Launching GoCardless in a new territory will require a depth and breadth of skills akin to starting a new company. It's an exciting challenge, and you'll benefit from the support and experience of our existing country leads. One of your first tasks will be to start building your team, hiring someone whose strengths complement your own to assist you as you forge a path for success in Belgium & the Netherlands.
