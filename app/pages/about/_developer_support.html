Developer Support

Solve our users’ technical problems and help us build products people want.

We pride ourselves on giving all our users first-rate support. Our simple, well-documented API is used by a growing number of our merchants and we are looking for someone versatile, capable and enthusiastic to join our support team to go the extra mile for developers building integrations with GoCardless.

Every day you will be:

  • Working as part of a close-knit team to provide legendary support
  • Acting as the first line for technical enquiries across multiple channels
  • Troubleshooting integration issues while supporting our products
  • Working with the engineering team to ensure that our service meets the highest standards

You'll have the chance to help us develop and improve our support tools and processes to benefit the Operations team as a whole. You will delve into the product on a technical level as you assist developers using GoCardless, and you'll get to help the first users of our incredible new Pro API. You'll also answer more general questions about the service and Direct Debit, and work on fraud detection and loss prevention processes. You should have an interest in programming and a desire to work on your coding skills while you're with us, even if you wouldn't consider yourself a developer yet.

We're a friendly, enthusiastic team and there are always opportunities to learn from everyone in the company. There's also a great office atmosphere, good coffee, tons of fruit and juice and a generous holiday allowance.

We’re looking for someone who:

  • Loves helping people
  • Has heaps of initiative, enthusiasm and patience
  • Has some programming experience and an appetite to learn more
  • Has a flawless command of English

Bonus points for previous experience at a start-up or in customer support.
