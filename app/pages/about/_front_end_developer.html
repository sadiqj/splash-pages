Front-end Developer

Help us create beautiful, intuitive experiences for our customers.

We believe that the last mile of design happens in the browser. We're looking for people that care as much about code quality as implementing great design. If you're the right fit, you probably see yourself as equal parts designer and developer.

On a day-to-day basis, you will be:

  • Working closely with our design team to create great user experiences
  • Helping our marketing team implement new ways of talking to prospective customers
  • Implementing new features in our core product and even building out entirely new products

We're looking for someone who:

  • Has good knowledge of HTML and CSS
  • Respects best practices and web standards
  • Understands and cares about the principles of design
  • Values precision

Bonus points for:

  • Formal design training and/or experience
  • Experience working with Marketing teams
  • Experience with Javascript and server side technologies

In your application, please include your CV and a link to your GitHub, as well as telling us about an interesting technical project or problem that you've recently worked on.
