Merchant agreement

GoCardless enables suppliers to let their customers pay securely by direct debit over the internet. We also allow suppliers to monitor the payments that they receive through GoCardless. This document sets out the terms of the contract ("Contract") between you, the supplier, and GoCardless for the payment services provided by GoCardless. These terms and conditions are available on our website and you may ask for a copy of them at any time while you have an account with us.

Any contract for the supply of goods and services between you and your customer is not affected by this Contract. GoCardless is not involved in the actual supply of any goods and services to the customer and any disputes should be resolved directly between you and the customer. If there is any difficulty with the goods or services you provide, you remain liable to the customer directly.

GoCardless cannot initiate refunds in respect of any goods and services you supply. We can, however, process them when you ask us to do so. If your customer lawfully cancels a payment in accordance with the GoCardless customer terms and conditions you must seek payment direct from your customer for any goods or services that you have provided to them.

When you sign up to GoCardless, you will need to provide us with certain information to create a GoCardless account. We will have to perform some identity checks on you.

By setting up your GoCardless account, you consent to us processing payments as detailed in this document (see Processing payments through GoCardless and Receiving payouts from GoCardless). We may apply limits for any payment transactions that take place through your GoCardless account. We will notify you of any limits and any changes that we make to them.

You are prohibited from doing any of the following:

  • selling, transferring or otherwise allowing third parties to access your GoCardless Account except as set out under "Permitted third party uses of your account"; and
  • collecting any customer's personal data that is accessed through your use of GoCardless without that customer's express permission

Permitted third party uses of your account

You may permit third parties you have contracted to use your account details in relation to that third party's provision of services to you, but you are responsible for all their actions and any misuse of the details as if it was your own.

In the event that you fail to comply with the terms of the Contract, or any instructions for use provided by GoCardless, we shall have no responsibility or liability to you for the performance of payment services.

You are responsible for the use of your account details by any other person to whom you have provided them or enabled their access to them. You must take reasonable steps to keep your account details safe and prevent fraudulent use of your account (and must ensure that any person to whom you disclose your account details takes the same reasonable steps).

Such precautions should include keeping your passwords, other access codes and any account related paperwork relating to the GoCardless payment services (“User Details”) in a safe place, confidential and secure from all unauthorised persons, not writing down your email address with password and taking reasonable care to ensure that other people do not oversee or hear you using your personal account details. You must inform us without undue delay on becoming aware of the loss, theft, misappropriation or unauthorised use of your account details. You will immediately terminate the access rights of any user who ceases to act in an authorised capacity on your behalf for any reason, including, for example, because of a change in employment status. You shall be solely responsible for (i) updating your passwords for access to the GoCardless payment services periodically, and (ii) creating passwords that are reasonably "strong" under the circumstances.

The User Details are the property of GoCardless and may be immediately revoked or terminated by GoCardless if you share the same with any third party, or otherwise breach the Contract.

You are prohibited from interfacing with the GoCardless API in a way which GoCardless has stated is not permitted in the current version of the GoCardless Documentation or the publicly available documentation relating to the system.

Processing payments through GoCardless

Upon receipt of a payment order from your customer we will process the payment, by direct debit, for the amount that your customer has agreed to pay for the relevant goods and services in accordance with the GoCardless customer terms and conditions.

We transmit each payment order made through a GoCardless account to the customer's bank to enable the settlement of each direct debit to occur on the agreed due date as calculated in accordance with these terms. Once a customer has a GoCardless account and direct debit mandate set up, it usually takes a maximum of 3 business days for us to receive money from the customer's bank account. If they are setting up an account as part of the payment, it can take an extra 2-4 business days to set up their direct debit mandate.

Funds collected from your customer's bank will be received into an account separate from our main operating accounts and allocated to your name within the GoCardless payment system.

Receiving payouts from GoCardless

Any funds that we receive from your customers will be allocated to your GoCardless account immediately before being paid out as detailed in this Contract. By entering into this Contract you are providing us with a payment instruction requesting us to execute a payment order seven business days after every transaction. The amount of funds held within your GoCardless account attributable to that transaction will be transferred to the bank account that you have nominated for these purposes, after we have made any authorised deductions (see Deductions, payments and invoicing).

You are not entitled to, and will not receive, any interest in relation to funds that are associated with your account within the GoCardless payment system.

Cancelling payment transactions

If for any reason you would like to cancel a payment from GoCardless account to you, you can do so at any time until the end of the business day before the day agreed to make the payment. You can cancel by notifying us by email to {{ GOCARDLESS.GB.SUPPORT.EMAIL }} or by phone using the contact details provided on the GoCardless website.

Deductions, payments and invoicing

You agree to pay GoCardless the applicable fees relating to each payment processed by GoCardless as notified to you from time to time. Our fees consist of any funds which accrue on the funds relating to any particular payment together with the fees set out on the GoCardless website our pricing section. All fees are due immediately and are non-refundable, except as otherwise expressly noted herein. You authorise GoCardless to deduct the applicable fees in respect of each payment when the relevant payment is made and funds are transferred from your GoCardless payment account to your bank account.

In addition to the fees mentioned in the above paragraph, GoCardless may also deduct amounts that take into account the following when a payment is made from your GoCardless payment account to your bank account.

  • Any applicable Affiliate Fees (explained in further detail below).
  • Any refunds that we have processed at your request.
  • Any chargebacks being made by your customers under the Direct Debit Guarantee or otherwise.
  • Any fees we have had to refund or costs we have had to incur as a result of payment transactions being cancelled.
  • Any unauthorised or incorrectly executed payment transactions notified to us by the customer plus any related interest and charges.

If you are using a service or product provided by another party (an "Affiliate"), such as a software provider or referral website which facilitates your use of GoCardless, they may be due an additional fee (an "Affiliate Fee") from you over and above our fee. If there is an Affiliate Fee, before you sign up to use the Affiliate's product or services, the Affiliate will notify you of any applicable Affiliate Fees. We will deduct Affiliate Fees from any sums payable to you and pay them to the relevant Affiliates.

Each time we make a payment to you, an entry for "GoCardless" will appear on your bank statement along with a reference number that is specific to that individual payment transaction. You can log in to your account at any time on the GoCardless website to view a summary of the payments we have made to you.

If the amounts which we are entitled to deduct (as set out above) exceed the amounts you are due to be paid by GoCardless, we may invoice you the difference. The amounts set out in any such invoice are due immediately and payable no later than 14 days after the date of the invoice. Where the amounts which GoCardless is entitled to deduct only arise after the Contract has been terminated, such as a chargeback under the Direct Debit Guarantee which is only made after termination, GoCardless is still entitled to invoice such sums to you notwithstanding termination of the Contract.

GoCardless will inform you (by making the information available when you log in to view your account or through the API) of any cancelled payment transaction as soon as reasonably practicable and in any event within 1 working day.

In the event that any of your customers do cancel their direct debit mandate we will be unable to process any payments that your customers may have committed to pay you through GoCardless.   5. a creditor or encumbrancer of that party attaches or takes possession of, or a distress, execution, sequestration or other such process is levied or enforced on or sued against, the whole or any part of its assets and such attachment or process is not discharged within 14 days;
  6. an application is made to court, or an order is made, for the appointment of an administrator or if a notice of intention to appoint an administrator is given or if an administrator is appointed over that party (being a company);

  8. a person becomes entitled to appoint a receiver over the assets of that party or a receiver is appointed over the assets of the other party;
