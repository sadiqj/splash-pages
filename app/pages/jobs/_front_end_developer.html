Front-end Developer

At GoCardless, we care deeply about creating great experiences for our customers. We are looking for talented front-end developers to help us craft these experiences.

We believe that the last mile of design happens in the browser. We are looking for people that care as much about the quality of their code as improving designs when implementing them. If you're the right fit, you probably see yourself as equal parts designer and developer.

On a day-to-day basis, you will be:

  • Working closely with our design team to create great user experiences
  • Helping our marketing team implement new ways of talking to prospective customers
  • Implementing new features in our core product and building out entirely new products

You should apply if you:

  • Have a strong knowledge of HTML and CSS
  • Have strong opinions about best practices and web standards
  • Care about and understand the principles of design
  • Have a pixel-perfect attention to detail

You'd be a particularly good fit if you have:

  • Formal design training and/or experience
  • Experience working with Marketing teams
  • Experience with Javascript and server side technologies
