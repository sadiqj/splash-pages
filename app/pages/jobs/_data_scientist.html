Data Scientist

We are looking for a data scientist to help us understand our customers' needs and shape our future marketing & product development.

You will initially work closely with our marketing team to build a repeatable and robust way of measuring our core marketing funnel. In the future, you will help us use data more effectively to make descisions across the business.

On a day-to-day basis, you will be:

  • Helping us to build out our analytics platform
  • Working closely with our marketing team to better understand our growth
  • Using our financial data to improve our anti-fraud measures

You should apply if you:

  • Have a degree in a quantitative science (e.g., Maths, Physics, Engineering, Economics)
  • Have experience querying large data sets (e.g., using SQL)
  • Are comfortable performing statistical analysis (e.g., using R, Matlab)
  • Are able to ask the right questions of data

You'd be a particularly good fit if you:

  • Have built analytics infrastructure for existing large data sets
  • Have experience with financial analysis and data visualization
  • Have experience with web programming langaugages

There’s very little hierarchy, a common hunger to learn, lots of freedom and all the responsibility you want to take. There’s also a great office atmosphere, good coffee, tons of fruit and juice, Friday beers and regular dinners out.
