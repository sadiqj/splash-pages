Why doesn't everyone use it?

There are some things you don’t want Direct Debit for. It’s not good for paying for your groceries, for example - payments don’t happen instantly, so your customer would already be home by the time you knew if the payment had been successful.

However for payments where the speed of payment doesn’t matter so much (like subscriptions and invoicing), it's a good idea: Direct Debit has less than half the transaction fees of card payments, and you never lose payments due to card expiry.
